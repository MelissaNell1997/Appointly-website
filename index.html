How It Works
Up and running in
under 30 minutes

No technical knowledge needed. We set it all up for you — you just send your clients the link.

01
📋
You tell us your details

Your business name, staff members, working hours, colours and logo. Takes 15 minutes over WhatsApp or email.

02
🎨
We build your branded portal

Your own subdomain — yourclinic.appointly.software — with your colours, logo, and staff. Looks and feels like your own custom app.

03
📱
Your clients book online

Share the link. Clients register, pick a staff member, choose a date and time, and book — 24/7 from any device.

04
🔔
Everyone gets notified

Clients get a WhatsApp and email confirmation. You get a full admin dashboard. Reminders go out automatically 30 minutes before.

Features
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for South African service businesses. No bloat, no complexity — just a system that works.

📅
Real-Time Availability

Clients see exactly which staff members are available and which slots are taken — no double bookings, ever.

💬
WhatsApp & Email Alerts

Automatic confirmation and reminder messages sent via WhatsApp and email. Set it once, forget about it.

🎨
Fully Branded Portal

Your logo, your colours, your business name. Clients never see "Appointly" — they see YOUR brand.

📱
Installs as a Phone App

Clients can add your portal to their phone home screen — it works exactly like a native app, no App Store needed.

📞
Manual Phone Bookings

Admin can capture walk-in and phone-in clients directly from the dashboard — all in one place.

🏥
Medical Aid Support

Capture provider, plan, and medical aid number at booking. Patient files ready before they arrive.

📊
Full Admin Dashboard

See today's schedule, all bookings, patient/client list, and cancellations — from any device, anywhere.

🔄
Reschedule Before Cancel

Clients are prompted to reschedule before cancelling — reducing no-shows and keeping your diary full.

🔒
Secure Staff Login

Admin portal is hidden behind a private password. Patients only see the booking flow — clean and professional.
